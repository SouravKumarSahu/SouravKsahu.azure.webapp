About Me

IT Analyst | IT Consultant | Azure Developer | Mainframe Developer | SWIFT | Investment Banking | Retail Banking

Ever since I was a little kid, I was very curious to look inside electronic toys to figure out its internal working. I was used to disassemble, assemble and modify these toys, back-and-forth. With this curiosity in heart, I pursued my engineering in electronics-telecommunications. This was for the first time I realized the power of the software behind the electronics, that I was used to play with. Hence, I decided to go for a career in software development.
Finally, now with 8 years of experience as an IT Analyst in businesses like Investments-Banking, Corporate-Actions, Cards-Payments and Retail-Banking, I am going through another very important realization in this industry.
"Technologies are just a means of achieving automated solutions to real-life problems and the true power lies in human intelligence, communication and cooperation."
Currently I am working on development & maintenance of mainframe applications, along migrating non-core application to Azure cloud platform. Hence, I have developed experience in a variety of technology stacks like IBM-mainframe, Azure-cloud, Visual COBOL, Rest API, Python, webapps and JAVA-backend. And yes, there is more to come and I am always open to learn.
